More About Me

Hi there! My name is Araceli Camarillo Estudillo, but many know me as Ara. I started coding in 2018 and have been discovering my place in the programming world ever since. In 2019 I joined Microverse Inc. where I have been completing my path as a Full Stack Developer, and where I have learned HTML, CSS, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, React, and Redux.

My goal is to find a remote job as a Ruby on Rails developer where I can learn & contribute to the workplace culture. I currently meet with the Women Who Code Community as well as the Cafe Con Ingenieras where we discuss the importance of women in the STEM industry and where we help each other strengthen our professional & coding skills.
